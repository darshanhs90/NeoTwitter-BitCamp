What We Do?

Enrich the user experience of the twitter application

Neo Twitter can be used for:

  • Enhancing your timeline with a much better UX
  • Modify the way you look at your tweets
  • Search your tweets using date,location and Content
  • Send group tweets to multiple people

This web app can easily be integrated into your twitter account. The future enhancements are

  • Pushbullet Feed
  • Pebble feed
  • Cell phone message feed
  • Add few more features to the web app
